NATASHA NORSKER

I was almost born in an elevator.
I grew up around Næstved.
I moved to Copenhagen.
I am studying my bachelors in Artificial Intelligence and Data.
I have been a tutor and very involved in the study start at DTU.
I am not a tutor anymore, but I made some of my very best friends through it.
I now work in a student driven non-profit organisation.
I also LOVE my job as a consultant of data analytics.
I draw and feel most like me when I am being creative.
I often go on long long walks while listening to music
Sometimes I read books that are not the usual heavy university textbooks.

Next

  1. January 2022: Conflux

    I started working at Conflux - a non-profit student organisation that aims to create a relationship between stem students in Denmark and people from businesses. We do this in two ways - firstly through our mentorship programme that connects a STEM student to a mentor and secondly through our consulting programme that let businesses present work cases the students can solve in teams.

  2. Aug 2020: Valtech_ - Commercial Strategy & Data Transformation

    I was hired as a student consultant in Valtech_ where I got a great mentor and was introduced to commercial thinking as well as a lot of BI and Data Science tasks.

  3. Feb 2020: Tutoring at DTU

    I was a tutor the entire year of 2020 and I loved it - so many cool new people that would become my friends and a lot of fun.

  4. Sep 2019: Bachelor in Artificial Intelligence and Data

    I was lucky enough to get admitted into my dream bachelor programme. I have not once regretted studying AI and DTU is the perfect place for it.

  5. XX 2018: IT camp for women

    The IT camp for women was hosted at SDU and introduced us to drag-and-drop programming and different programming competitions.

  6. XX 2016: Project Forskerspirer

    Now you can sit back and relax, everything from here on out is a bonus! Expect more great memories as you head south, you may get lucky and spot a Polar Bear hunting a Seal.

  7. Sep 2016: Tutor at Næstved High School

    I tutored the new students that started high school in the year of 2016 and introduced them to the school.

  8. XX 2015: UNF Physics Camp

    The summer of 2015 I went to a physics camp and met some cool people.

  9. XX 2014: Started Næstved High School

    While going to high school I was active in the student counsil as well as did some extracurricular activities like being a host at some physics shows the school had. I also took physics masterclass.
